Hi! I'm Vanika

Software Developer, Sales Engineer, Social Introvert


Hello! I'm a Field Sales Engineer at Intel in Dallas, Texas. How did I get here? Keep reading!

  • 1. Graduated from the University of Colorado Boulder in 2018 with a Bachelor's in Electrical and Computer Engineering, a minor in Computer Science, and a certificate in Leadership.
  • 2. Throughout college, I had internships at DragonDev (a local start-up), Hunter Douglas, and Intel Corporation. I was also continually involved in the Society of Women Engineers (SWE) working my way up from a member to a Director of Outreach, Society Representative, and eventually to Vice President. I was also involved in the South Asian Student Association, the Electrical Engineering Society, the Engineering Honors Program, and Engineering Leadership Program.
  • 3. After an incredible internship experience at Intel Fort Collins in SoC Validation, I received a full-time offer as a IP Validation Engineer!
  • 4. Joined CXLCM Pre-Silicon IP Validation Team in Hillsboro, Oregon, an open-source cache-memory accelerator.
  • 5. After 2 years on the job, I decided to make a shift to the business side of things; started a position as a Field Sales Engineer.

My interests include hardware design (microcontrollers, FPGAs, IP/SoCs), as well as low-level software development (C/C++/Verilog). My favorite part of these technologies is talking about it! Collaboration! I will never forget the long nights in college staying up to finish Digital Logic projects with the entire class.

Outside of work, I enjoy hiking, reading, trying new foods (#Imissbrunch), and getting to know new people! I am also trying to teach myself web development (hence this website).
