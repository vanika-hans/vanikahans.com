Hi! I'm Vanika

Software Developer, Sales Engineer, Social Introvert


Hello! I'm a Field Sales Engineer at Intel in Dallas, Texas. How did I get here? Keep reading!

  • 1. Graduated from the University of Colorado Boulder in 2018 with a Bachelor's in Electrical and Computer Engineering, a minor in Computer Science, and a certificate in Leadership.
  • 2. Throughout college, I had internships at DragonDev (a local start-up), Hunter Douglas, and Intel Corporation. I was also continually involved in the Society of Women Engineers (SWE) working my way up from a member to a Director of Outreach, Society Representative, and eventually to Vice President. I was also involved in the South Asian Student Association, the Electrical Engineering Society, the Engineering Honors Program, and Engineering Leadership Program.
  • 3. After an incredible internship experience at Intel Fort Collins in SoC Validation, I received a full-time offer!
  • 4. Moved to Hillsboro, Oregon to work on Pre-Silicon IP Validation for CXLCM, an open-source cache-memory accelerator.
  • 5. Decided to shift to the business side of things and gain a perspective of the industry as a whole. Applied my engineering background and communication skills to my role as a Field Sales Engineer where I covered accounts in Wireless, Cloud, Flash Storage, and Audio/Video industries.
  • 6. After gaining an understanding of the industry as a whole, I decided to go back to my engineering background and apply my skillset to an industry I am really passionate about: Life Sciences. I am now a Solution Architect a part of Intel Lab and Life Sciences.
